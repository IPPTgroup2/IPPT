2. User Roles


The IPPT offers multiple user roles, including:
Admin:
User with administrative privileges responsible for system management.
Personnel:
Individuals who use the system to log their attendance (time in and time out) and approve service requests.
Student/Visitors
Users who can identify the presence of personnel real-time. Moreover, can request services offered by the institution and receive notifications regarding their requests.

3. QR Code Attendance Tracking


The IPPT enables personnel to generate QR codes for attendance tracking purposes. The QR code system provides real-time information about personnel presence on campus.

4. Service Requests


Users, including students and visitors, even admin can submit service requests through the IPPT. The personnel will review and process these requests, and users will receive notifications regarding the approval or rejection of their requests.

5. Reports


The IPPT System will generate reports, including attendance records of personnel and the status of service requests that have been accommodated. These reports are provided for record-keeping purposes that may be used in future matters.

6. Privacy and Data Security


User data such as First name, Middle name, Last name, Email, Faculty No., Course, Passwords, including attendance records and service requests, will be handled in accordance with R.A. 10173 also known as Data Privacy Act, to protect user information.

Users of the IPPT are responsible for:

-Providing accurate and truthful information.
-Safeguarding their login credentials.
-Complying with all information needed.

The developers may update or modify the IPPT System's features, functionality, or Terms at their discretion. Users will be notified of significant changes.
